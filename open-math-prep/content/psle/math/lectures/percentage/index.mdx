### Intro to Percentage

<Youtube id={"AL0-0f9azNo"} />

Percentage is basically a special fraction, where the denominator is 100. Just convert to fraction and divide, multiply accordingly. Just take note not to add two percentages of different things together.

Example: 50% of A and 25% of B -> dont do 50% + 25%
