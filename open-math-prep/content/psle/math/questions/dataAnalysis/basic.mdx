      </div>
      <div className="step">

        <p>Total score = 82 × 4 = 328</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Calculate New Total Score</h3>
        <p>New total score = Initial total + New student's score</p>
        <p>New total score = 328 + 90 = 418</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Calculate New Average</h3>
        <p>New average = New total score ÷ New number of students</p>
        <p>New average = 418 ÷ 5 = 83.6</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Final Answer</h3>
        <p>The new average score is 83 (rounded to the nearest whole number).</p>
      </div>
    </div>

      </div>
      <div className="step">

        <p>Combined average = Combined total ÷ Total students</p>
        <p>Combined average = 2340 ÷ 30 = 78</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Final Answer</h3>
        <p>The combined average score is 78.</p>
