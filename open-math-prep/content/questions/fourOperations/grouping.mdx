### Question 1

<Callout title="Question">
  Mark bought an equal number of shorts and shirts for 100 dollars. A shirt cost 8 dollars and each pair of shorts cost 12 dollars. How much did he spend on the shirts?
</Callout>
<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Answer Walkthrough">
  <p className="ml-11">Ans: $40</p>
  <div className="steps">
    <div className="step">
      <h3>Step 1: Group 1 shirt and 1 pair of shorts</h3>
      <p>$8 + 12 = 20$</p>
      <p>Each group (1 shirt + 1 pair of shorts) costs <span className="text-blue-500">$20</span></p>
    </div><div className="step">
  <h3>Step 2: Find the number of groups</h3>
  <p>$100 \div 20 = 5$</p>
  <p>Mark bought <span className="text-green-500">5 groups</span> of shirt and shorts</p>
</div>

<div className="step">
  <h3>Step 3: Find the amount spent on the shirts</h3>
  <p>$5 \times 8 = 40$</p>
  <p>Mark spent <span className="text-red-500">$40 on shirts</span> <span className="text-purple-500">(Answer)</span></p>
</div></div>
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
