### Question 1

<Callout title="Question">
  There are 836 students in a school. 7/10 of the boys and 7/8 of the girls take
  bus to school. The number of boys who do not take bus is twice the number of
  girls who do not take bus. How many girls do not take bus?
</Callout>
<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Answer Walkthrough">
    <p className="ml-11">Ans: 57</p>
    <div className="steps">
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Find the fractions of boys and girls who do not take bus</h3>
        <p>
          Boys: $1 - \frac{7}
          {10} = \frac{3}
          {10}$
        </p>
        <p>
          Girls: $1 - \frac{7}
          {8} = \frac{1}
          {8}$
        </p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Compare the boys and girls who do not take bus</h3>
        <div className="flex flex-wrap space-between">
          <EqualPartPieChartComponent title={"Boys"} slices={3} />
          <EqualPartPieChartComponent title={"Girls"} slices={1} />
        </div>
        <p>
          $\frac{3}
          {10}$ of boys = $2 \times \frac{1}
          {8}$ of girls
        </p>
        <p>
          $\frac{3}
          {10}$ of boys = $\frac{1}
          {4}$ of girls
        </p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Make the numerators the same (split to equal slices)</h3>
        <p>
          Make numerator for girls 3: $\frac{1}
          {4} = \frac{3}
          {12}$
        </p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Compare the denominators</h3>
        <p>Boys : Girls = $10 : 12 = 5 : 6$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>
          Find the total number of units and equate it to the total boys and
          girls
        </h3>
        <p>$5u + 6u = 11u$</p>
        <p>$11u = 836$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Find 1 unit</h3>
        <p>$1u = 76$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Find the total number of girls</h3>
        <p>$6u = 456$ (Total girls)</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Find the girls who do not take bus</h3>
        <p>
          $\frac{1}
          {8} \times 456 = 57$ girls (Answer)
        </p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
### Question 2
<Callout title="Question">
  In a library, 3/4 of the number of fiction books is equal to 1/6 of the number
  of non-fiction books. There are 5940 fiction and non-fiction books altogether.
  How many fiction books are there in the library?
</Callout>
<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Answer Walkthrough">
    <p className="ml-11">Ans: 1080 fiction books</p>
    <div className="steps">
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Compare the boys and girls who do not take bus</h3>
        <div className="flex flex-wrap space-between">
          <EqualPartPieChartComponent title={"Fiction Books"} slices={3} />
          <EqualPartPieChartComponent title={"Non Fiction Books"} slices={1} />
        </div>
        <p>
          $\frac{3}
          {4}$ of ficton books = $\frac{1}
          {6}$ of non-ficton books
        </p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Make the numerators the same (cut up non fiction books)</h3>
        <p>
          Make numerator for non fiction books 3: $\frac{1}
          {6} = \frac{3}
          {18}$
        </p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Compare the denominators</h3>
        <p>Fiction : Non Fiction = $4 : 18 = 2 : 9$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>
          Find the total number of units and equate it to the total boys and
          girls
        </h3>
        <p>$2u + 9u = 11u$</p>
        <p>$11u = 5940$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Find 1 unit</h3>
        <p>$1u = 270$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Find the total number of fiction books</h3>
        <p>$4u = 1080$ (Total fiction books)</p>
      </div>
    
    </div>
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
