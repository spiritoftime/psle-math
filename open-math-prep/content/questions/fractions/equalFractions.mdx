### Question 1

<Callout title="Question">
  There are 836 students in a school. 7/10 of the boys and 7/8 of the girls take
  bus to school. The number of boys who do not take bus is twice the number of
  girls who do not take bus. How many girls do not take bus?
</Callout>

<Accordions>

  <Accordion title="Answer Walkthrough">
    <p className="ml-11">Ans: 57</p>
<div className="steps">
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Find the fractions of boys and girls who do not take bus</h3>
    Boys: $1 - \frac{7}
    {10} = \frac{3}
    {10}$<br />
    Girls: $1 - \frac{7}
    {8} = \frac{1}
    {8}$
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Compare the boys and girls who do not take bus</h3>
    <div className="flex flex-wrap space-between">
  <EqualPartPieChartComponent title={"Boys"} slices={3} />
  <EqualPartPieChartComponent title={"Girls"} slices={1} />
</div>
    $\frac{3}
    {10}$ of boys = $2 \times \frac{1}
    {8}$ of girls<br />
    $\frac{3}
    {10}$ of boys = $\frac{1}
    {4}$ of girls
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Make the numerators the same</h3>
   Make numerator for  girls 3: $\frac{1}
    {4} = \frac{3}
    {12}$
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Compare the denominators</h3>
    Boys : Girls = $10 : 12 = 5 : 6$
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>
      Find the total number of units and equate it to the total boys and girls
    </h3>
    $5u + 6u = 11u$ <br />
    $11u = 836$
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Find 1 unit</h3>
    $1u = 76$
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Find the total number of girls</h3>
    $6u = 456$ (Total girls)
  </div>
  <div className="step">
    <h3>Find the girls who do not take bus</h3>
    $\frac{1}
    {8} \times 456 = 57$ girls (Answer)
  </div>
</div>
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
