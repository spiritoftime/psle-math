        <p>
          $y = \frac{17}
          {7} \approx 2.43$
        </p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Solve for x</h3>
        <p>Substitute $y = 2$ into Equation 1:</p>
        <p>$x + 2(2) = 7$</p>
        <p>$x + 4 = 7$</p>
        <p>$x = 3$</p>
      </div>
      <div className="step">
        <h3>Final Answer</h3>
        <p>$x = 3$, $y = 2$</p>
